## 基础概念/词汇
* Securitization: 将整个的loan打包了
* recourse loan: 有追索其他财产的权利
* non-recourse loan: 没有追索权
* strategic default: 房子的价值小于贷款 
* conforming mortgage: a loan satisfies the underwriting standard
